This Elliston tall two-piece toilet combines classic architectural elements with bold contemporary lines to create a design that works well in a wide range of bathroom decors. A full 2-1/2" taller than Comfort Height toilets, it provides the ultimate in accessibility and ease of use. With a water-saving 1.28-gallon flush, the Elliston tall toilet also features Revolution 360 flushing technology to keep your bowl clean longer than a conventional flush. For added convenience, The Complete Solution package comes with all the necessary installation parts.

  • Elongated bowl offers added room and comfort

  • Tall height offers seating 2-1/2" (63.5 mm) higher than Comfort Height for enhanced comfort, ease of sitting and standing, and gracious accessibility

  • AquaPiston canister optimizes the flow out of the tank, harnessing gravity and the natural powerful reducing cascade of water to increase the power and effectiveness of the flush

  • Revolution 360 flushing technology generates a forceful swirling motion to keep the bowl clean longer

  • Sanitary guard where bowl and tank meet for ease of cleaning

  • Quiet-Close lid and seat prevents slamming

  • Grip-Tight bumpers hold seat firmly in place

  • Precision-engineered patented tank, bowl, and trapway create strong siphon during flushing

  • Left-hand Polished Chrome trip lever
